How to Divide Numbers

Here are instructions for using the division feature of the JPS Calculator app.

To successfully divide one number by another, you must be familiar with the arithmetic process of division.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the divide (รท) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.
The result of the division is displayed.
You want to divide 6 by 2. The equation looks like this: 6 รท 2 = 3. You enter 6, press the divide button, enter 2, and press the equals button. The result 3 appears on the screen.
